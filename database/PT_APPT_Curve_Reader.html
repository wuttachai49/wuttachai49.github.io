Mixing Study — Inhibitor vs. Deficiency

Mix patient plasma 1:1 with normal pooled plasma. Repeat immediately and after 2 h incubation at 37°C.
Correction → Factor deficiency (VIII, IX, XI, XII). Proceed to specific factor assay.
No correction → Inhibitor. Confirm lupus anticoagulant with dRVVT or hexagonal-phase phospholipid test.
