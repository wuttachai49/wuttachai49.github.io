🔄 Basic Gating Strategy

Step 1: Gate singlets (FSC-A vs FSC-H) → exclude doublets
Step 2: Gate live cells → exclude dead cell markers (PI/7-AAD)
Step 3: Gate CD45+ leukocytes → all white blood cells
Step 4a: T cells → CD3+ → CD4+ (Helper) or CD8+ (Cytotoxic)
Step 4b: B cells → CD19+ or CD20+ (separate from T cells)
Step 4c: Monocytes → CD14+ (use FSC/SSC for separation from lymphocytes)
